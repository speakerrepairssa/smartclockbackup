## Medium Bio (250 words)

**SmartClock** is a comprehensive cloud-based SaaS platform that revolutionizes workforce management for businesses of all sizes. Built on modern Firebase technology, SmartClock bridges the gap between physical biometric attendance devices and cloud-based analytics to deliver end-to-end automation of time tracking and payroll processing.

**How It Works:**  
Employees clock in and out using Hikvision biometric devices (fingerprint or face recognition). Attendance data syncs to the cloud in under 1 second via secure webhooks. Business owners instantly see who's working through a real-time dashboard that updates automatically without page refresh.

**Automated Payroll:**  
SmartClock automatically calculates hours worked, overtime, and net pay based on hourly rates. At month-end, the system generates professional, branded payslips using a QuickBooks-style visual editor and delivers them instantly via WhatsApp Business API or email. No manual calculations. No errors. No hassle.

**Key Features:**
- Real-time employee status monitoring (IN/OUT)
- Automated timecard generation with overtime tracking
- Professional payslip designer with logo and brand colors
- WhatsApp and email delivery
- Shift management and compliance tracking
- Multi-tenant architecture for businesses with multiple locations
- Assessment cache for instant payroll calculations

**Perfect for retail, manufacturing, hospitality, healthcare, and service industries** where accurate time tracking and efficient payroll processing are essential. SmartClock reduces payroll processing time by 80%, eliminates calculation errors, and provides complete transparency to both employers and employees.
